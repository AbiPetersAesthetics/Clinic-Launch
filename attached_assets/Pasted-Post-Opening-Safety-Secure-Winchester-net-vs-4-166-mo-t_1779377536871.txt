Variable = Winchester stock %, commissions %, marketing, staffing, consumables. Fixed (Winc) = all items from your fixed cost list including dual costs (counted once, not double-charged to Bedhampton).

Bedh Net = Bedhampton gross revenue minus stock, running costs, and Bedhampton's share of VAT. Winc VAT = Winchester VAT only. Net Profit = Winc Rev − Variable − Fixed − Winc VAT + Bedh Net.

Proj costs = Project Plan task costs (mid-tier by default) charged this month. Hover to see which tasks. Tasks without due dates are spread across pre-opening months, weighted toward opening. Total across all months = £42,993.

Drawings = Owner's drawings taken from the business once Winchester is self-funding. Capped so at least £3,000/month is always retained in the business. Set your target in Assumptions → Personal & Runway.
