TIER 1 — BLOCKS EVERYTHING ELSE. Must be done before or during fit-out. No later than end of September.
These are either legal requirements without which the clinic cannot trade, or procurement items with long lead times that will physically block the fit-out or opening if not ordered in time.
Clinical governance — Abi owns, non-negotiable:
BLS Certification — confirm current. This is first because if it's lapsed it takes time to renew and she cannot treat patients without it. Do this week, not 1 September.
Hamilton Fraser / indemnity / premises insurance — must be confirmed and extended to cover the Winchester address before a single patient is seen there. Do not open without this. Target 2 October but check now whether the insurer needs notice of the new address ahead of that.
Adverse Event Protocol — written, signed, in the clinic. Required for insurance and Save Face.
NMC Face-to-Face Prescribing SOP — legally required given Abi is an independent prescriber administering POMs. Cannot treat toxin patients without this.
IPC Policy — infection prevention and control. CQC inspection standard. Non-negotiable.
Sharps Safety Policy — legal requirement under Health and Safety (Sharp Instruments in Healthcare) Regulations 2013. Fine risk if absent.
Consent Forms and Questionnaires — Winchester setup. Cannot treat a single patient without these in place for the new location. Do not assume Bedhampton versions automatically carry over — they need to reference the Winchester address and any updated protocols.
Complaints and Incident Log — must be in place from day one. CQC standard.
Batch Number and Traceability Log — medicines traceability is a legal requirement. Must be in place before first toxin or filler treatment.
Emergency Drugs and Equipment Kit — including adrenaline for anaphylaxis. Cannot legally treat injectable patients without this on site.
Clinical Waste Contract — must be in place before first clinical treatment. Sharps and clinical waste cannot go in general waste. Lead time on clinical waste contract is typically 2-4 weeks — do not leave this to October.
Fire Risk Assessment — must be completed after fit-out is finished and before opening. Book the assessor now for 20-23 October slot.
