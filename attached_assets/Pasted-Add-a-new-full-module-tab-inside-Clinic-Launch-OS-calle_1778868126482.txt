Add a new full module/tab inside Clinic Launch OS called “Competition Intelligence”.

This tab should help assess whether there is enough market space for Abi Peters Aesthetics to open at a selected clinic property, starting with 9A Jewry Street, Winchester.

This should NOT be a basic competitor list.

It should feel like a full commercial market intelligence dashboard for a medical aesthetics clinic launch.

CORE PURPOSE:
Answer the question:

“Is there genuinely room for Abi Peters Aesthetics in this location, and who are the biggest threats?”

The system must compare every competitor against Abi Peters Aesthetics, not just list them.

Abi Peters Aesthetics positioning baseline:
- nurse-led medical aesthetics clinic
- Advanced Nurse Practitioner / Independent Prescriber-led
- natural results
- medical professionalism
- premium but approachable
- strong trust/social proof
- established clinic moving from Bedhampton into Winchester
- likely positioning: premium medical aesthetics, not beauty salon
- core treatments: anti-wrinkle, dermal filler, skin boosters, polynucleotides, microneedling, premium skincare, future advanced skin/laser
- strong marketing/automation capability
- target client: safety-conscious, natural-results-focused, professional female demographic

24. IMPORTANT SAFETY / LEGAL REQUIREMENTS
====================================================

Do not make defamatory or unsupported claims about competitors.

Where information is not verified, label it clearly:
- “Not clearly evidenced online”
- “Unclear”
- “Not found”
- “Requires manual verification”

Do not claim someone is unqualified unless verified by official sources.

Avoid inflammatory language.

The goal is strategic analysis, not criticism.
