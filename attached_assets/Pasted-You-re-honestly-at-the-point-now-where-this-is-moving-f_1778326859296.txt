For 9A Jewry Street specifically, here’s what I think is still missing or underrepresented:

1. AIR CONDITIONING / HVAC
Missing / important

You mentioned AC in passing but it needs formal treatment.

Add:

Task	Suggested Cost
HVAC inspection/service before occupation	£250 / £500 / £1,500
Additional AC unit if required	£0 / £2,500 / £6,000

Why:

injectables rooms get HOT
Winchester clientele expect comfort
poor HVAC kills premium feel
FRI lease may make AC your problem

This is one of the biggest hidden commercial-unit costs.

You partly covered this but not fully operationally.

Need:

temperature logging SOP
backup thermometer
power outage procedure
fridge calibration

Tiny cost.
Huge compliance importance.

7. CASHFLOW BUFFER

This is the biggest strategic point.

You need a dedicated line:

Task	Suggested

Not fit-out money.
Operating survivability.

Because the real pressure becomes:
