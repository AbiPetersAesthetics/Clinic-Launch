Build a comprehensive Risk Register page for the Launch OS. This is a standalone page accessible from the main navigation, titled "Risk Register". Full specification below.
DATA SOURCE — AUTO-POPULATION
On page load, automatically extract and categorise all risks from the following existing data sources within the model:

All tasks marked HIGH or CRITICAL risk in the Project Plan (all phases)
All warning flags from the Financial Modelling tab (VAT threshold, Bedhampton mismatch, capacity overstatement, October cash low point)
All amber and red items from the AI Launch Recommendation (financial, property, market, strategic, life design scores)
All compliance items marked NOT STARTED in the CQC & Compliance tab
All non-negotiable conditions from the lease negotiation strategy
All ramp risks from the 12-month revenue forecast
Life Design flags (school run conflicts, nursing exit timing, undocumented family logistics)
Any items in the Optimisation tab flagged as CRITICAL on LOW cost tier

Each auto-extracted risk should be tagged with its source so the user knows where it came from.
RISK STRUCTURE
Each risk entry must contain the following fields:

Risk ID (auto-generated, e.g. R001, R002)
Risk Title (short, plain English)
Risk Description (2-3 sentences — what could go wrong, when, and what triggers it)
Category (Financial / Legal & Lease / Clinical & Compliance / Operational / Life Design / Market & Competition / Strategic)
Likelihood (1-5 scale: 1 = Rare, 2 = Unlikely, 3 = Possible, 4 = Likely, 5 = Almost Certain)
Impact (1-5 scale: 1 = Negligible, 2 = Minor, 3 = Moderate, 4 = Major, 5 = Critical)
Risk Score (Likelihood × Impact, auto-calculated, displayed as a number and colour: 1-4 green, 5-9 amber, 10-14 orange, 15-25 red)
Residual Likelihood (1-5, user sets when treatment action is written)
Residual Impact (1-5, user sets — impact rarely changes but sometimes does)
Residual Score (auto-calculated, same colour banding)
Risk Reduction (auto-calculated: "Score reduced from X to Y — Z% reduction")
Treatment (four options: Treat / Tolerate / Transfer / Terminate — selectable pill buttons)
Treatment Action (free text — what specifically, by whom, by when)
Owner (dropdown: Abi / David / Solicitor / Accountant / Agent / Contractor / Insurer / External)
Due Date (date picker)
Status (Not Started / In Progress / Mitigated / Closed)
Pipeline Stage (Pre-Lease / Lease Signing / Fit-Out / Pre-Opening / Month 1-3 / Month 4-6 / Month 7-12 / Ongoing)
Linked Model Section (auto-tag showing source)
Linked Risks (multi-select field — tag related Risk IDs. e.g. R001 → R004, R007. Display as clickable chips that jump to the linked risk entry)
Watch List (star toggle ☆/★)
Score History (auto-recorded each time likelihood or impact changes — stores previous score and date)
Last Reviewed Date (updated on any edit or manual mark-as-reviewed)

PAGE LAYOUT
Section 1 — Risk Dashboard (top of page)
Show summary tiles:

Total risks (count)
Critical risks scoring 15-25 (red, count)
Risks with no owner assigned (amber)
Risks due in next 30 days with no treatment action (red)
Overdue treatment actions — risks past due date with status not Mitigated or Closed (red alert)
Risks trending upward in last 7 days (amber — deteriorating risks)
Total inherent risk score vs total residual risk score with overall reduction percentage
Number of risks where residual score equals inherent score (no mitigation taken yet)

Show a 5×5 risk matrix — likelihood on one axis, impact on the other. Each cell shows count of risks at that score. Clicking filters the register.
Show risk appetite indicator — slider with three positions: Conservative (flag 6+) / Moderate (flag 9+) / Aggressive (flag 12+). Default: Conservative. Risks above threshold show "Above appetite" label. Risks within threshold show "Within appetite."
Show Watch List widget — top 5 watch list risks with current score, residual score, and last reviewed date. Header turns red if any watch list risk not reviewed in 30 days. Show "Trending Up" sub-section — any watch list risks whose score increased in last 7 days.
Show Mitigation Value panel:

Total inherent score (sum across all risks)
Total residual score (sum)
Overall reduction percentage
Count of risks with no mitigation taken

RISK SEGREGATION — TAB BAR
Add a top-level tab bar with the following distinct views:

All Risks
Project & Delivery
Financial
Legal & Lease

Each tab shows a mini summary bar: count, highest score, number unaddressed, RAG status for the category.
PRIORITY ORDER
Within every view, risks display in strict priority order:

Primary: Risk Score descending
Secondary: Pipeline Stage ascending (earlier stages first on equal scores)
Tertiary: Impact descending

Show visible priority rank number on each row (#1, #2, #3 etc.) across the whole register. Updates automatically when scores change.
RISK REGISTER TABLE
Full sortable filterable table. Default sort: Risk Score descending. Filterable by Category, Treatment, Owner, Status, Pipeline Stage, Risk Score band.
Columns visible by default: Rank / Risk ID / Title / Category / Inherent Score / Residual Score / Trend / Treatment / Owner / Due Date / Status / Watch List star.
Each row expandable — full detail including description, treatment action, linked risks, score history, residual scoring panel, and escalation flag.
Inline editing for Treatment, Treatment Action, Owner, Due Date, Status, Likelihood, Impact, Residual Likelihood, Residual Impact — no separate edit screen.
Show both inherent and residual score columns so mitigation progress is visible at a glance.
TREATMENT LOGIC
When treatment is selected, show contextual guidance:

Treat: "Define specific actions to reduce likelihood or impact. Assign an owner and due date."
Tolerate: "Accept this risk as within appetite. Document why and review at next milestone."
Transfer: "Identify who takes this risk — insurer, solicitor, landlord, contractor. Document the mechanism."
Terminate: "This activity or decision is abandoned to eliminate the risk entirely. Document the decision." Auto-set residual score to 0 and display "Risk eliminated."

ESCALATION FLAG
If residual score after mitigation remains at 12 or above, automatically flag the risk with a red "Escalate" badge and the message: "Residual risk remains high after treatment — re-treat or escalate to decision maker." This prevents risks appearing mitigated on paper when they have barely moved.
RISK INTERDEPENDENCIES
The Linked Risks field on each entry allows tagging of related Risk IDs. Display linked risks as clickable chips. When a risk is opened, show a "Cascade Warning" panel if any linked risks are currently scoring above appetite threshold: "If this risk materialises, the following linked risks may also be triggered: [list]." This allows Abi and David to see cascade effects before a risk event occurs.
WATCH LIST
Star icon on every row. Starred risks appear in Watch List tab. Watch List shows:

All starred risks in priority order
Last reviewed date per risk
Review due indicator — amber if not reviewed in 14 days, red if not reviewed in 30 days
One-click "Mark as reviewed" button
Summary: "X risks on watch — Y need review"

RISK TREND
Every time likelihood or impact changes, record previous score and date. Show trend indicator on each row:

↑ red — score increased since last edit
↓ green — score decreased
— grey — unchanged

Dashboard shows "Trending Up" section — risks whose score increased in last 7 days — so deteriorating risks surface automatically.
OWNER NOTIFICATIONS
On the dashboard and within each category tab, surface an "Action Required" panel showing:

All risks where an owner is assigned, a due date is set, and no treatment action has been written — flagged as "Treatment action missing"
All risks where due date is within 7 days and status is not Mitigated or Closed — flagged as "Due soon"
All risks where due date has passed and status is not Mitigated or Closed — flagged as "Overdue" in red

These should be filterable by owner so David can view his actions and Abi can view hers separately.
PIPELINE VIEW
Dedicated sub-view titled "Pipeline View" — timeline of risks grouped by Pipeline Stage from today through month 12 post-opening. Each stage shows:

Count of risks active in that stage
Highest scoring risk
Count with treatment action vs unaddressed
RAG status for the stage

Add "Horizon Scan" panel at the bottom showing the three highest-scoring unaddressed risks in each upcoming pipeline stage. Label: "Coming up the pipeline — act before these become active."
ADD CUSTOM RISK
Simple form to add risks not auto-generated. All fields as per risk structure above. On save, appears immediately in register tagged as "Manual."
AUTO-REFRESH
On navigation to the Risk Register, auto-check for new HIGH or CRITICAL items in Project Plan, Compliance tab or Financial Model since last load. Prompt: "X new risks detected from updated model data — add to register?" One-click confirm.
RESIDUAL RISK PANEL
On every risk entry, second scoring panel below inherent score titled "Residual Risk — after mitigation":

Residual Likelihood (1-5)
Residual Impact (1-5)
Residual Score (auto-calculated, colour banded)
Risk Reduction display: "Score reduced from X to Y — Z% reduction"

When Tolerate or Transfer selected: "Set residual score to reflect what remains after tolerance/transfer."
When Terminate selected: auto-set residual to 0, display "Risk eliminated — activity abandoned."
When residual score remains 12+: show red Escalate badge.
EXPORT
Export button generates formatted PDF section styled with existing Launch OS export. Includes:

Watch List as highlighted section at front
Full risk register with inherent and residual scores
Mitigation value summary
Horizon scan for next two pipeline stages
Risks trending upward in last 7 days flagged explicitly
Cascade warnings for any linked risk clusters above appetite threshold

Insertable as Section 9A in the main full Launch OS export.
