Replit Project Brief — “Clinic Launch OS”
Version 1.0
Prepared for: Abi Peters Aesthetics Ltd
Product Goal:

Build a professional web-based operational platform that combines:

Commercial property evaluation
Financial modelling
Clinic launch project management
Build cost optimisation
Risk management
Timeline management
AI-assisted property analysis
Document intelligence
Decision tracking

Into a single “Clinic Launch Operating System”.

This is NOT a generic project management app.

The app should feel like:

a clinic acquisition advisor
a commercial lease planner
a build-out coordinator
a financial modeller
and a launch command centre

all in one.

1. CORE PRODUCT VISION

The platform exists to answer one question:

“Can this clinic location realistically become a profitable, low-risk aesthetics clinic — and if so, how do we launch it properly?”

The system should:

analyse commercial units
assess location viability
estimate profitability
model cashflow risk
manage the entire launch process
optimise costs intelligently
track every task and dependency
produce actionable outputs

This should feel:

executive
premium
operational
strategic
data-driven
